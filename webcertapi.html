Certificate Management Protocol

the certificate management protocol follows by RFC4210. this specification describe the issue, revoke, renew and re-issue of certificate as followings:

  • Certificate Enrollment
  • Revoke of Certificate
  • Renew of Certificate
  • Re-Enrollment of Certificate
